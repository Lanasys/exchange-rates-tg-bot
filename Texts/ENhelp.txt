This bot recognizes numbers and currencies in text and automaticaly replies with converted ones.

Exchange rates are updated once in a couple of hours: API: http://data.fixer.io/api/ (Currencies)
Exchange rates of crypto are updated once in a couple of seconds:  API: https://api.binance.com/api/v3/ (Crypto)

Latest version supports all world's currencies and 14 cryptocurrencies: ADA, BCH, BNB, BTC, DASH, DOGE, ETC, ETH, LTC, RVN, TRX, XLM, XMR, XRP.

Use \about    for more info about authors, version, source code and/or license.

Use \settings to configure privileges or to change output currencies.

Please, if you find a mistake in text recognition, use \wrong to report an issue.

In case of other troubles, consider writting directly to developers.
